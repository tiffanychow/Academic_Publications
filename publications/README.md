## Technical Capabilities

* **Machine Learning and Pattern Classification:** Implementing supervised classification such as regularized logistic regression (RLR), multi-voxel pattern analysis (MVPA), searchlight mapping, cross-validation, and evaluations of performance (area under the curve and receiver operating characteristics; AUC-ROC).
* **Custom Data Pipelines and Feature Engineering:** Creating bespoke preprocessing scripts and workflows to standardize large, noisy datasets, including extraction of relevant features, controlling for confounding variables, and engineering novel analytic approaches.
* **Longitudinal and Time-Series Analytics:** Conducting linear mixed-effects modeling with regressions, individualized trajectory extraction, heteroscedasticity management, and irregular time-series alignment.
* **High-Dimensional Multimodal Data Integration:** Cross-analyzing behavior with neuroimaging data obtained through multi-tracer molecular PET (Amyloid-β and Tau), MRI, fMRI, and tf-fMRI.
* **End-to-End Experimental Design (National Science Foundation Funded):** Designing multi-week primary research protocols with naturalistic, real-world visual stimuli to probe cognitive states and architecting data collection specifically to enable downstream predictive modeling and machine learning, as supported by a National Science Foundation (NSF) Graduate Research Fellowship.
* **Data Mining and Cohort Analytics:** Mining complex, pre-existing databases to discover hidden relationships across high-dimensional neuroimaging and behavioral features, leveraging cohort stratification, regression, and posthoc testing frameworks (Type-II ANOVA).
* **Commercial Translation:** Mapping biological and behavioral endpoints onto proxy metrics, forecasting individual trajectories and developing potential early-warning algorithms, and defining product strategy for health tech and digital therapeutics.
